Welcome to DCA (Dark Coderz Alliance), hope you like what we try to bring to all of you, if you don't know our MANIFEST then give me some moments to give you a brief idea of what we are and what we do.

First DCA was founded by me 'Sinclaire' and was built with the help of my friends not members only but all the people who supported us and promoted us *All Greetings To Them* and our plan was simple give out new ideas, new techniques and new methods, snippets, libraries anything which can help to others in their journey of the VX side also our goal was to give out sources to Viruses/Worms for every level of coders not only the advanced ones but the n00bes too ;) *Isn't that great or what* and the idea was also not how complex we can code but what can we give to all of you to learn and to actually benefit from *Don't You Just Love Me :)* and also not the typical ASM work but any other languages that can actually get the job done (i.e. Viruses/Worms) so we tried to spread in every programming language and anything we saw that can make the world not a better place ;) and also not for WIN platforms only but for *NIX too :), and most thing that makes us different than others is that the QUALITY is what we are concerned about not the QUANTITY, and also as everyone knows there aren't many active groups now in the scene anyway here are the languages we support:

  • Assembly (TASM/FASM/NASM)
  • C/C++
  • Delphi
  • Visual Basic
  • Visual Basic Scripts
  • Batch
  • .NET (Very Soon...)

You can also find us in IRC, but you will have to follow some rules in order to stay there those rules can be found here also you can check out the list of people who are actually in the DCA channel (#DCA) and friends to DCA too :) this list can be found here too.
